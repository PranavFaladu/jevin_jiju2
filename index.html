About Company

Established in 1982, Stonex Ceramics has been at the forefront of the tile industry for over 40 years. With unwavering energy and dedication to our commitments, we have continuously evolved to meet changing market demands while maintaining our core values of quality and innovation.

Our transformative and novel ideas have revolutionized the ceramic industry, earning us numerous honorary awards. We take pride in our remarkable position in the global ceramic market and remain dedicated to serving our valuable customers who appreciate our commitment to excellence.

At Stonex Ceramics, we combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to create tiles that not only meet but exceed international standards. Our passion for design and quality drives us to constantly push boundaries and set new benchmarks in the industry.
